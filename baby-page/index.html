How does it work?

Any contributions made go to a Square Cash account for funding an Edvest 529 educational savings plan set up and controlled by the parents.

After contributing with a credit or debit card, you will get a receipt from Square confirming your contribution. Then, the parents send you an mail to confirm that they're transferring the money into their 529 investment account.

What's a 529 Plan?

A 529 plan is a tax-advantage education savings plan designed to help young parents save for the mounting costs of college and higher education.

Any contribution made now will grow tax-free in an Edvest 529 investment account, and all the money in this account is required to be used for ualified educational expenses.

Making a gift to the plan now will allow your contribution to multiply as it grows tax free.
